Why Motorbikes Are Popular

Motorbikes are widely used because they are fuel-efficient, affordable, and easy to maneuver in traffic. They are popular for both daily commuting and long-distance touring.

Popular Motorbike Types

Common types include sport bikes, cruisers, dirt bikes, and scooters. Each type serves a different riding purpose.
